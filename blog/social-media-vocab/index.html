Social media vocab deficit

2013-01-27

What should they be called, those people who add their thoughts to the bottoms of web pages?

Users? A bit cold and vague. Commenters? Commentators? The first is ungrammatical, the second loaded with connotation. Community members? Surely too earnest by half.

As if the word "content" were not bad enough, the English language cannot even provide a decent name for the guys who create it for free.
