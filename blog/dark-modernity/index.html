Dark modernity

2015-05-16

There is something strangely revolting about the case of Dzhokhar Tsarnaev.

Here is a refugee who abused political asylum to terrorize the citizens of his host country. A murderer convinced of his righteousness by the irrational blusterings of uneducated religious zealots he saw on YouTube. And a lazy, narcissistic, incurious American college student with an apparently tenuous appreciation of the rights of others, including their right to live.

Such a lot of modern ills in one person.
