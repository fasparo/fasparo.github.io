Identity scoping

2012-12-15

Are we Europeans or are we just Brits and Germans and French and the rest? Are we simply Europeans or are we world citizens and people? Are we just people or are we one species (humanity) among countless others, all dependent on each other? Identity needs to be scoped upwards, fast.
