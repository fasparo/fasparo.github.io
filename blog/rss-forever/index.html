RSS forever

2013-09-14

RSS, the dumbest-named idea since the Ford Edsel. And also one of the best. Here are the three reasons RSS beats Twitter without a fight.

  1. RSS contains only content, not fluff. And if you use it for its original purpose – keeping up with individual bloggers – you'll usually get the whole content without needing to visit the site.

  2. With RSS you can timeshift. Who wants to read news backwards? That's what you're doing with Twitter. By tracking what you've read already, RSS allows you to consume news in the order it was produced.

  3. Unlike Twitter, RSS belongs to you. You choose which app to use, and where your personal data gets stored. You are not a product, but an autonomous user.
